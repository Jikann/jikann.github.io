About

I love playing games and I love solving problems. When it comes to most games, I'm not necessarily looking to master them, but I am looking to understand the mechanics and get a grasp for the systems that underlie them. To that end, I play a wide variety of games in many different genres and try to strictly judge them based on how those systems work and how much I enjoy them.

I also enjoy learning about languages and writing systems. I know both Spanish and Japanese to at least an intermediate (though not necessarily fluent) level and frequently look up other languages for fun.
